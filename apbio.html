Course Overview

Mrs. Heapy's AP Bio Online is equivalent to a first-year college level biology course. We focus on advanced biological concepts and rigorous preparation for the College Board Examination.

Class Schedule

Wednesdays 4:00 – 5:00 PM
Includes Lectures, Hands-on Labs, and Office Hours.

Prerequisites

  • HS Biology (Ecology, Cell Division, Genetics)
  • Algebra 1 (Min. B+ Grade with graphing/stats)
  • Recommended: Chemistry or AP Chemistry

Course Fees

Tuition: $850

Textbook: $100 - $200

Basic Lab Kit: $70 (Domestic US only)

Main Topics

Macromolecules Cell Energetics Heredity Molecular Genetics Evolution Biotechnology Statistics Chi Square Water PropertiesBiological systems and Regulation

*Chemistry fundamentals (pH, polarity, ions) are expected as prior knowledge.

Investigative Labs

  • Building respirometers for oxygen production
  • pH & Temperature effects on enzymes
  • Molecular movement across membranes
  • Measuring mitotic rates via staining
  • Photosynthesis & CO2 production rates

Materials Needed

  • Campbell Biology (13th Edition)
  • Scientific/Math Calculator
  • 100-page Lab Book (Physical or Teacher-approved Digital)
  • Standard graph paper & supplies
Note: If you do not have any experience with advanced STEM courses, I recommend you take AP Chemistry before this course or a preparatory Honors Biology course offered by AP Homeschoolers. For young students, a phone call will be required before accepting application for enrollment.
